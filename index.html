Rushit N. Shah
PhD, Computer Science · University of Illinois, Chicago

I work on problems in imitation learning, reinforcement learning, and optimization.

My dissertation introduced a satisficing perspective on imitation learning, using subdominance minimization as a unified objective for learning acceptable behavior from suboptimal demonstrations.

I have also worked on fault detection algorithms for aerospace systems.
