Biography

PhD student in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence in the Computer Science Department at the University of Illinois at Chicago.

My research interests include developing safe AI through imitation learning, optimization, and causal inferencing. I have also worked on developing algorithms for early fault detection in aerospace systems.
