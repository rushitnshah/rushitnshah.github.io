Chicken/Turkey/Lamb Keema

Back to Home
Hands-on Time 15 mins
Total Time 45 mins

This is a simplified version of the full recipe here (use it as reference while cooking).

Ingredients

  • Protein: Minced chicken/turkey/lamb (~250gms)
  • Veggies: Lettuce
    • 1 onion (chopped)
  • Spices:
    • ½ tsp Cumin
    • ¼ tsp Turmeric
    • 1½ tsp Any garam masala
    • 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
    • Lemon juice
  • Optional: 1 Tomato (chopped/mashed), Green peas (eyeball it), Coriander (for garnish).

Steps

  1. Heat 2-3 tbsp oil in a pan (medium low heat).
  2. Add cumin, wait 10-20 seconds till it crackles.
  3. Add onions and saute for 3-4 mins.
  4. Add ginger garlic paste (use a lid! oil WILL splatter) and let cook for 30 seconds.
  5. If using tomatoes and green peas, add at this point and cook covered till tomatoes turn mushy.
  6. Add remaining dry spices and saute for 1 min.
  7. Add minced meat and salt and mix evenly with onion/tomato mash. The meat should start to crumble–keep breaking large chunks into smaller ones.
  8. Cook covered on low flame until all moisture is released and has evaporated.

How to Eat

Best enjoyed with any type of bread, roti, or even rice.
