(Baked) Chicken Caesar Salad w/ Pasta

Back to Home
Hands-on Time 15 mins
Total Time 45 mins

Ingredients

  • Protein: Chicken (Breast or Thighs)
  • Veggies: Lettuce
  • Spices:
    • Salt/Pepper
    • Garlic powder
    • Any chicken/taco seasoning
  • Pasta: Any kind (I use Barilla+ protein pasta).
  • Caesar Dressing:
    • 1/3 cup yogurt
    • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
    • 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
    • Garlic powder
    • Salt/pepper

Steps

  1. Season chicken with spices and bake @ 350°F for 30-45 mins (don’t bother cutting beforehand). Chop roughly once cooked3.
  2. Boil your pasta and chop your lettuce.
  3. For the Caesar dressing, mix the ingredients. Adjust to your taste by adding more yogurt/mayo/lemon juice.

How to Eat

  1. Prepare the chicken/pasta/dressing/lettuce chopping when meal-prepping. Mix everything just before eating.
  2. Reheating chicken: for best results, quickly toss in a pan with a little oil.
3. If chicken appears uncooked after chopping, put back in over for 15 mins. Not all footnootes are aggresive.↩
