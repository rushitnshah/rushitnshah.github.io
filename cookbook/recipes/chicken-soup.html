Chicken and Veggie Soup

Back to Home
Hands-on Time <10 mins
Total Time 45 mins

This is a simplified version of the full recipe here (use it as reference while cooking).

Ingredients

  • Protein: Chicken (Any body part, breast if you like pulled chicken in soup)
  • Veggies: Literally Any veggies you have on hand – go crazy!!! I use frozen mixed veggies.
    • Suggested: Onions, Potatoes (regular), Carrots, Celery, Broccoli, Cauliflower, Green Peas, Green Beans, Mushrooms.
  • Spices:
    • Salt/Pepper
    • Garlic Powder
    • Any other seasonings (I use the first three seasonings I can find)
    • (Optional) Chicken/beef bouillon powder or bone broth.

Steps

  1. Heat 2-3 tbsp olive oil in a POT and add ALL ingredients.
  2. Cover everything with enough water (or a mix of water and bone broth/bouillon) and boil until everything is cooked.
    • I (still) dgaf if your chicken and veggies are frozen! Just throw everything in there.
    • DO NOT CHOP YOUR CHICKEN – THROW IT IN AS IT IS !!!! YOUR GOAL IS TO SAVE TIME !!!!!
  3. Remove chicken from soup, chop/shred, and add back to soup:
    • Breast: use two forks to shred.
    • Thighs/other parts: chop.
  4. Enjoy with a slice of toasted sourdough bread
