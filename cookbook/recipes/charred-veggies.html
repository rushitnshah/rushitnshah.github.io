Charred Veggies & Chicken w/ Tzatziki

Back to Home
Hands-on Time 10 mins
Total Time 60-75 mins

Ingredients

  • Protein: Chicken (any part)
  • Veggies: Literally Any veggies you have on hand – go crazy!!! I use frozen mixed veggies.
    • Suggested: Onions, Potatoes (regular or sweet), Carrots, Broccoli, Cauliflower, Green Peas, Green Beans, Mushrooms.
  • Spices:
    • Salt/Pepper
    • Garlic powder
    • Any other seasonings (I use taco seasoning)
  • Tzatziki:
    • 1 cup yogurt
    • (Optional) grated cucumber

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Throw all your ingredients onto a baking sheet. Drizzle olive oil, throw the spices in, toss all that shit around, and throw in the oven. It’s not rocket science.
    • Idgaf if your chicken and veggies are frozen, throw everything in as it is. This recipe was meant to save you time: don’t be stupid and try to defrost a week’s worth of veggies and chicken. A 350°F oven WILL take care of defrosting.
  3. For the Tzatziki, mix yogurt, olive oil, salt, garlic powder (and optionally grated cucumber).
